WIN · PLACE · SHOW
The simplest bets at the track. Start here.
WIN
Your horse finishes 1st.
Example: $5 WIN on BROCK (horse #3)
Pays if #3 wins. Nothing otherwise.
AT THE WINDOW
$5 WIN on number 3
Typical cost: $2 minimum, bet any amount
• Hardest to hit
• Biggest payout per dollar
PLACE
Your horse finishes 1st or 2nd.
Example: $5 PLACE on BROCK (horse #3)
Pays if #3 finishes in the top 2.
AT THE WINDOW
$5 PLACE on number 3
Typical cost: $2 minimum
• Easier than Win
• Smaller payout
SHOW
Your horse finishes 1st, 2nd, or 3rd.
Example: $5 SHOW on BROCK (horse #3)
Pays if #3 finishes in the top 3.
AT THE WINDOW
$5 SHOW on number 3
Typical cost: $2 minimum
• Easiest to hit
• Smallest payout
The Value Play: Furlong's EDGE % tells you when a horse is being underpriced. A horse with +20% edge on a WIN bet means the crowd thinks it's a 10-1 shot when the model says it should be 6-1. That's where you find profit.

THE EXOTICS
Bigger payouts. More combinations. Still manageable.
EXACTA
Pick the 1st and 2nd place finishers.
STRAIGHT
Exact order. Horse A wins, Horse B places.
$2 EXACTA — NICK over CASEY (1st then 2nd)
Cost: $2 · Harder to hit · Bigger payout
BOX
Either order. One wins, the other places.
$2 EXACTA BOX — NICK + CASEY
Cost: $4 (2 combos × $2) · Easier · Smaller payout
Box 3 horses: $12 (6 × $2) · Box 4: $24 (12 × $2)
TRIFECTA
Pick the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishers.
STRAIGHT
Exact order. Hardest to hit, massive payout.
$1 TRIFECTA — NICK, CASEY, ROGER (exact)
Cost: $1
BOX
Any order across your horses.
$1 TRIFECTA BOX — NICK + CASEY + ROGER
BOX 3: $6 (6 combos) · BOX 4: $24 (24 combos)
BOX 5: $60 (60 combos) · BOX 6: $120 (120 combos)
A $1 SIX-HORSE TRIFECTA BOX covers every possible 1-2-3 finish among those 6 horses.
SUPERFECTA
Pick the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th place finishers.
BOX 4: $1 base = $24 BOX 5: $1 base = $120 BOX 6: $1 base = $360
Furlong's ticket builder scales your base bet to fit your budget automatically.
BOX = coverage, STRAIGHT = precision More horses in the box = more combinations = more cost Build My Ticket handles all the math
