INTRODUCING FURLONG
Finding value the public misses.
A 336-point scoring engine that identifies undervalued horses, builds optimized tickets, and tells you exactly what to say at the betting window — all offline, on any device.
We don't predict who wins.
We find horses the public is undervaluing.

Every bet at the track is priced by the crowd. When the crowd is wrong about a horse, the payout is bigger than it should be. Furlong finds those horses — then builds smart tickets to capitalize. No other platform combines multi-factor scoring, live value detection, and automated ticket construction in a single offline-capable tool.

◆ 336-Point Scoring Engine
Every horse scored across 15 weighted categories — speed, class, form, connections, pace, post position, equipment, and more. No opinions, no gut calls. Pure mathematical assessment before you see a single result.
▲ Live Value Detection
Edge % compares our fair-odds model against what the crowd is actually betting. Positive edge means the public is sleeping on a horse — and you should be paying attention. This is the signal no other platform gives you.
◼ Ticket Builder with Kelly Sizing
Set a budget, pick a risk mode, and Furlong allocates your bankroll across the highest-value plays — Win, Exacta, Trifecta, Superfecta. Every ticket comes with a script you read verbatim at the window.
● Zero-Dependency Offline Architecture
Runs entirely on-device. No server calls, no wifi requirement, no subscription gating. Load a DRF file from any North American track and the entire platform runs at full capability with zero network connectivity.
★ Transparent Methodology
Every score is decomposed into factor-level breakdowns. Every recommendation is explainable. Every ticket is verified against program numbers. Nothing is a black box.

THE EXOTICS
Exactas, Trifectas & Superfectas
Bigger payouts. More combinations. Still manageable.
EXACTA
Pick the 1st and 2nd place finishers.
STRAIGHT
Exact order. Horse A wins, Horse B places.
$2 EXACTA — NICK over CASEY (1st then 2nd)
Cost: $2 · Harder to hit · Bigger payout
BOX
Either order. One wins, the other places.
$2 EXACTA BOX — NICK + CASEY
Cost: $4 (2 combos × $2) · Easier · Smaller payout
Box 3: $12 (6 × $2) · Box 4: $24 (12 × $2)
TRIFECTA
Pick the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishers.
STRAIGHT
Exact order. Hardest to hit, massive payout.

Cost: $1
BOX
Any order across your horses.

BOX 5: $60 (60 combos) · BOX 6: $120 (120 combos)
A $1 SIX-HORSE TRIFECTA BOX covers every possible 1-2-3 finish among those 6 horses.
