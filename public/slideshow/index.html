INTRODUCING FURLONG
Finding value the public misses.
A 336-point scoring engine that identifies undervalued horses, builds optimized tickets, and tells you exactly what to say at the window — all offline.
We don't predict who wins.
We find horses the public is undervaluing.

Every bet at the track is priced by the crowd. When the crowd is wrong, Furlong finds those horses and builds smart tickets to capitalize. No other platform combines multi-factor scoring, live value detection, and automated ticket construction in one offline tool.
